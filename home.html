Our Services

Automatic Irrigation System

Our automatic irrigation system leverages a precise water level sensor that continuously monitors your water reservoir. Once the tank is detected to be full, the system seamlessly allows water to enter, ensuring a consistent supply. This intelligent process minimizes manual oversight and prevents overflow, guaranteeing efficient and reliable irrigation for your crops.

Soil Moisture Monitoring

With our advanced soil moisture monitoring solution, sensors are embedded in the field to measure the moisture content in real time. When the soil begins to dry, the system promptly activates the irrigation process to draw water from the tank. This dynamic approach not only conserves water but also fosters optimal hydration, ensuring your plants thrive with the ideal moisture balance.

Greenhouse Rain Protection

To protect your crops from unpredictable weather, our greenhouse is equipped with a state-of-the-art rain sensor. As soon as rain is detected, the system automatically closes the greenhouse, preventing excess water from entering and damaging your valuable plants. This proactive measure maintains a stable environment and safeguards your investment during inclement weather.

Climate Monitoring

Utilizing the DHT11 sensor, our climate monitoring system continuously tracks ambient temperature and humidity. By integrating this climatic data with soil moisture readings, the system makes precise decisions on when to dispense water. This ensures that your crops receive just the right amount of hydration based on both weather conditions and soil needs, optimizing growth and reducing waste.

Our upcoming AI-driven rodent detection system uses an ESP32-CAM module to monitor your farm in real time. The system will identify rodent activity using machine learning algorithms and alert farmers immediately, helping to prevent crop damage and maintain a pest-free environment.

Weather Prediction

By leveraging AI-powered weather prediction models, our system will provide real-time forecasts tailored for farmers. These predictions will help in making informed decisions about irrigation, harvesting, and crop protection against extreme weather conditions.
